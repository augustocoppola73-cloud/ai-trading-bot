# Strategy Template

Use this template when documenting a strategy.

## Strategy Name

Short descriptive name.

## Intent

What market behavior should this strategy capture?

## Inputs

Required data columns, indicators, and parameters.

## Entry Logic

Explicit BUY conditions.

## Exit Logic

Explicit SELL, stop, trailing stop, or rotation conditions.

## Risk Rules

Sizing, stop distance, volatility filters, and max exposure.

## Metrics To Watch

Relevant performance and risk metrics.

## Failure Modes

When this strategy is expected to perform poorly.
