# Trade Timeline

The current portfolio timeline visualizes weekly dynamic portfolio rotation.

## Current Design

The y-axis is selected ticker. The x-axis is scan date.

Hover context includes:

- Reason.
- Weeks held.
- Weekly return.
- Capital.
- Scanner score.
- Volatility.

## Purpose

The timeline helps answer:

- When did the portfolio rotate?
- Why did it rotate?
- How long were positions held?
- Did the rotation coincide with score or volatility changes?
