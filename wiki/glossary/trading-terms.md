# Trading Terms

## ATR

Average True Range. Used here as a volatility measure and stop-distance input.

## BULL

Market regime where short or medium trend is positive according to project
indicator rules.

## BEAR

Market regime where trend conditions are negative according to project indicator
rules.

## CASH

Portfolio state where no asset is selected.

## Drawdown

Decline from previous peak equity or capital.

## EMA

Exponential moving average. Used here to compare short, medium, and long trend.

## PnL

Profit and loss.

## RSI

Relative Strength Index. Used here for momentum and overbought filtering.

## Scanner Score

Composite score used to rank assets by AI score, regime, signal, trend distance,
and volatility penalty.

## SIDEWAYS

Market regime where indicator rules are not clearly bullish or bearish.

## Slippage

Assumed execution cost modeled by worsening entry and exit prices.
